Frequently asked questions

What is Tendo Pay?

Tendo Pay is an instant credit solution that gives you the convenience to buy now and pay later for your online purchases in 30 days, 2 months, 3 months, 4 months or 5 months installments (3.95% interest per month).

How can I start using Tendo Pay?

There are 2 ways to do it:

  • a. Create an Account on www.tendopay.ph in few simple steps.
  • b. Select Tendo Pay as the payment method while checking out on one of Tendo Pay e-commerce partners.

What is required to set up an account on Tendo Pay?

In order to start using Tendo Pay, you need to:

  • a. Be at least 18 years old and possess one of the officially accepted IDs (to find the full list please refer to the question below)
  • b. Have a valid e-mail address
  • c. Have an active Facebook account
  • d. Provide us with your full name and date of birth

When can I make my first purchase with Tendo Pay?

After setting up your account, we will need to verify your identity and evaluate your Tendo Loan application. For the verification purposes we will need to complete following steps

  • a. Send us a Clear Photo of one of the one of the officially accepted IDs (to find the full list please refer to the question below)
  • b. Provide us a Proof of the valid address in Metro Manila (we will need a photo of a document confirming your address, to find the full list of accepted documents, please refer to the question below).
  • c. Provide us a Valid e-mail address and Philippines mobile phone number
  • d. Complete short test, which will help us evaluate your credit profile
When you complete all of the above, give us a while, and we will come back to you with credit decision as soon as possible. If you are approved, you can start shopping with Tendo!

What are the officially accepted IDs?

We accept following IDs:

  • Armed forces of the Philippines (AFP) ID
  • Driver’s license
  • Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) e-card Plus2
  • National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) clearance
  • Certification from the National Council for the Welfare of Disabled Persons (NCWDP)
  • OFW E-card
  • Overseas Workers Welfare Administration (OWWA) ID
  • Passport
  • Police Clearance Certificate
  • Postal ID
  • Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) ID
  • Seaman’s Book
  • Senior Citizen Card
  • Social Security System (SSS) Card
  • Unified Multi-Purpose ID
  • Voter’s ID

How can I prove my address?

We will need a photo of one of the following documents, so that we can verify your address:

  • Bank Statement (within the last 6 months)
  • Credit Card Billing Statement (within the last 6 months)
  • Utility Bill (water, electricity, cable, phone - within the last 6 months)
  • Mobile phone Bill (within the last 6 months)
  • Barangay Clearance/Barangay Certficiation of Residency
  • Income Tax Return (Form 1700 or 1701)
  • Mail from a University, Government Institution or Embassy
  • University Transcript of Records (Within the last 6 months)

Do I need a bank account or credit history to start using Tendo Pay?

You don't need bank account or credit history to use Tendo Pay.

What are the fees associated with using Tendo Pay

There is no upfront fees for using Tendo Pay. You will be charged 3.95% monthly interest rate. If you are late paying for any payment to Tendo Pay, there will be a 4% per day late fee on top of 3.95% interest per month.

What is the limit for purchases with Tendo Pay?

The minimum purchase you can make using Tendo Pay must be above 2000 PHP The initial credit limit you can use for your purchases will be 10000 PHP. While you build your credit history with Tendo Pay by paying on time we will be increasing your limit to maksimum 80000 PHP.

How can I repay my Tendo Loans?

While buying through Tendo Pay you will be asked one of listed repayment outlets which is most convenient for you:

  • 7eleven
  • Cebuana Lhuillier
  • MLhuillier
  • Palawan Pawnshop
  • Debit/Credit Card
  • Bank Transfer: BDO, BPI, Land Bank, Metrobank
  • Bank Deposit: BDO, BPI, Land Bank, Metrobank
  • Coins.ph
  • GCash
  • Paymaya

You can always change your prefered repayment outlet on your Tendo Pay app.

Is Tendo Pay available in all cities in the Philippines ?

As for now Tendo Pay can be used by residents of Metro Manila, but we are working hard to make it available in other regions soon.

Is my personal information secure with Tendo Pay?

Yes, protecting your personal information is our top priority. We encrypt all sensitive data. We also provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Where can I shop with Tendo Pay?

You can find list of partner merchants on our website. We are constantly growing our community to make your online purchases more convenient.

How do I return and get a refund for my purchases?

Return of goods depends on the return policy of the respective merchant. Please contact the merchant if you wish to return your goods. Tendo Pay will only make a refund if the merchant receives your goods back.

Will Tendo Pay send me a repayment remainder?

Yes, we will send you several SMS reminders regarding your upcoming payment.

What will happen if I do not pay my loan?

If you do not pay your instalment on the due date, your account will be blocked until you pay outstanding amount. You will continue to get a reminder to pay as long as you do not settle your outstanding balance. If you fail to pay, we are entitled to take action under your loan agreement such as the use of third party debt collection services and / or the reporting of your non-payment actions to credit bureau.

How long does approval process take

Approval process will take maximum 1 business day from the moment you submitted all required documents.
