How it works

Shopping online made easy

Tendo Pay is like a credit card without the plastic. It’s the perfect way to spread the cost of your online purchases into multiple installments.

Sign Up

Sign up and submit your application easily online. We'll notify you by email if you're approved.

Get Approved

We review your application and get back to you with an approval and your account details.

Go Shopping

Go shopping online! The merchant sends you your product and your purchase is split into installments over time on a schedule that works for you.

Buy at a cost you can easily understand

Your payments and interest are clearly visible. No hidden or additional fees. Get full transparency on your payment plan.

Timing that works for you

Select timing you're comfortable with. Between our multi-month options you will get to select a re-payment structure that fits your lifestyle.
